Message Feature

These messaging tools promote efficient and effective processes. Through the school messaging system, schools can improve communication flow with all stakeholders: teachers, students, employees, and parents/guardians. A good flow of communication helps institutions meet their goals and fulfill their mission.

Benefits

  • Improved Information Dissemination: Messages, announcements, and news can be circulated quickly. The feature supports bulk notices, targeted messages, and file sharing so everyone stays informed.
  • Document Exchange: Keep records of important conversations and files. Stakeholders can access shared documents for academic purposes.
  • Accessible to Stakeholders: Responsive on mobile and desktop. Teachers can broadcast from laptops; students and parents receive messages on mobile.
  • Helps in Strategic Planning: Collaborate on exams, schedules, and class activities. Better communication supports stronger workflows.
  • Targeted Audience: Filter recipients by class, stream, subject, or role so notices reach the right people.
  • Data Backup: Messages are stored in the app for future reference and audits.

SMS delivery uses Twilio when configured. Email delivery uses SMTP. If either is not set, the system will still send in-app messages only.
