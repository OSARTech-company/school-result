Terms of Use and Privacy Notice

Last updated: March 8, 2026

1. Platform Scope

  • This platform is operated by Osartech LTD for school management workflows.
  • Registration is school-only: only approved schools can be onboarded by Osartech LTD or an authorized super admin.
  • Individual self-registration is not allowed for personal use outside a registered school.
  • It is for authorized users only: super admin, school admin, teacher, student, and parent roles.
  • Access and actions are limited by role permissions configured in the app.

2. Acceptable Use

  • Keep your username, password, and access device secure.
  • Do not share account credentials or bypass access restrictions.
  • Do not upload harmful files, offensive content, or unauthorized data exports.
  • You are responsible for actions performed using your account.

3. Data We Process

  • Identity data: names, role, username, and class/school associations.
  • Academic data: scores, attendance, disputes, and report records.
  • Operational data: timetable, messages, audits, and issue reports.
  • Security data: login attempts, role-based actions, and error logs.

4. Privacy and Access Control

  • Data access is role-based and restricted to legitimate school operations.
  • User accounts are provisioned under a registered school tenant; standalone individual accounts are not created.
  • Parents only see linked-child data; teachers only see assigned class/subject scope.
  • System logs are used for security, compliance, and troubleshooting.
  • We do not permit unauthorized disclosure of student/staff records.

5. Data Retention and Security

  • Records are retained according to school policy and legal requirements.
  • Backups, audits, and integrity checks may be used to preserve system reliability.
  • Users should report suspected breaches immediately via Report Issue.

6. Consent

By checking the Terms and Privacy box at login, you confirm that you have read and agreed to these terms. This acceptance is recorded per user account and may be required again after major policy updates.

7. Contact

For policy or support questions, contact your school administrator first. If needed, escalate through the in-app Report Issue page to Osartech LTD support.

For data privacy actions (export, deletion, correction), use the in-app Privacy Request form.
